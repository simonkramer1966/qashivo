0) Core Product Principles (Non-Negotiable)

Qashivo operates on supervised autonomy:

The system recommends

Humans approve

Nothing executes automatically

UX must reduce anxiety:

White space

Clear status

Minimal CTAs

This is portfolio control, not practice management

Partners are operators, not just admins

Language must be accountant-calm:

No hype

No urgency

No “AI magic” language

Log audit event

8.2 Invite Email (Exact Copy — Do Not Paraphrase)

Subject:
“{PartnerFirm.name} has invited you to Qashivo”

Body:

Hi {FirstName},

{PartnerFirm.name} has invited you to use Qashivo, a credit control and cashflow visibility platform they use to support clients.

Qashivo helps manage outstanding invoices in a structured, supervised way — capturing real customer responses (such as promises to pay or disputes) so cashflow expectations are clearer and chasing is more focused.

Nothing happens automatically.
All actions are reviewed and approved by your accountant or their credit control team before anything is sent.

Once you accept:

You’ll connect your accounting system (read-only receivables access)

You’ll confirm who Qashivo should contact

Your accountant will approve the first action plan

Accept invitation

You stay in control at all times. You can disconnect Qashivo whenever you wish.

Introduced by {PartnerFirm.name}
Powered by Qashivo

13) Definition of Done

The MVP is complete when:

A partner can invite an SME

SME accepts, consents, and connects accounting

Partner sees SME status change clearly

Credit controller sees only their clients

Branding applies consistently to UI + email

No customer communications execute automatically
