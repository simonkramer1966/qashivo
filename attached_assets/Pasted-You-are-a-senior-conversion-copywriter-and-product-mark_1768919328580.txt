You are a senior conversion copywriter and product marketer. Your job is to rewrite the copy for Qashivo’s marketing website so it aligns to “v1” product truth and tone.

Context (v1 truth from the app):
- “Qashivo is continuously managing receivables. No action is required unless something is flagged in Attention.”
- The product centers on: continuous monitoring, automated follow-up, promise tracking, attention/exceptions workflow, and cash inflow / cash position forecasting with confidence ranges.
- Visual design is already nailed: calm, minimal, whitespace-first. Copy must match that restraint.

Tone & style:
- Calm, CFO-grade, specific, non-hype.
- Avoid competitor-bashing, superlatives (“revolutionary”, “only platform”), and vague “AI magic” claims.
- Prefer concrete verbs/outcomes: “monitors”, “nudges”, “tracks promises”, “flags risk”, “updates forecast”.
- Headlines: 6–10 words. Subheads: 1–2 short sentences. Use plain language.
- Use one primary CTA: “Book a demo” (or “Get a demo”). Secondary CTA: “See how it works” / “View the product”.

Deliverables:
1) Homepage copy rewrite with section-by-section output:
   - Header nav labels (5–7 items max)
   - Hero (H1, subhead, CTAs, 1 trust line)
   - Social proof strip (headline + placeholder for logos)
   - 3-value-prop tiles (title + 1 sentence each)
   - Cash Forecast section (heading, subhead, 3 bullets)
   - Collections Performance section (heading, subhead, 3 bullets)
   - “How it works” 3-step section (step title + 1 sentence each)
   - Integrations section (heading + subhead)
   - Security/compliance section (heading + 2 bullets, no fake claims)
   - Final CTA section (heading, subhead, CTAs)
   - Footer headings (group labels only)

2) Product page copy (module-based) that mirrors the app navigation:
   - Overview, Attention, Cash Flow, Follow-ups/Promises
   For each module: heading, 1–2 sentence body, 3 bullets.

3) Pricing page copy:
   - Page hero (H1 + subhead)
   - 3 plan names (neutral, CFO tone) with 3 bullets each (avoid pricing numbers)
   - 6 FAQs (question + 2–3 sentence answer) including:
     “What does Attention mean?”, “How long to see value?”, “What integrations do you support?”, “Can we customize follow-ups?”, “How does forecasting work?”, “Is there an implementation fee?”

4) Microcopy swaps:
   Provide 10 “Before → After” examples that replace hype with v1 clarity.

Inputs you should request (only if missing):
- Current site copy (paste or screenshots)
- Target audience (SMB, mid-market, agencies, etc.)
- Primary integrations (e.g., Xero, QuickBooks, Netsuite)
- Compliance/security facts (SOC2, ISO, etc.)—do not invent

If inputs are not provided, proceed with reasonable placeholders and clearly mark them like: [Integration A], [Logo], [Security detail].

Output format:
- Use clear section headers.
- Provide 2 alternative hero options (A/B) and 2 alternative final CTA options.
- Keep everything ready to paste into a CMS.

Now produce the rewritten copy.
