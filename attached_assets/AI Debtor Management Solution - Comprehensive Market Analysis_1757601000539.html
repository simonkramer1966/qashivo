Construction businesses represent our ideal customer profile because they experience the most severe payment cycle problems among all SMB industries. This makes them the highest-value, most motivated segment for AI-powered debt collection solutions.

  • Longest payment cycles: 60-90 days (vs 30-45 days other industries)
