Perfect — we’re designing one of the most important dialogs in Qashivo: the Invoice Detail View, which acts as a command centre for each invoice.
Here’s a world-class, investor-impressing UI/UX design concept that works beautifully across mobile and desktop.

🧾 QASHIVO — INVOICE DETAIL DIALOG
🧠 Design Philosophy

Clean, modular, and responsive — inspired by Apple’s card-based hierarchy.
Every key action should be available in two taps or less.
On mobile: vertical scroll with collapsible sections.
On desktop: two-column layout with action buttons pinned to the right.

💡 STRUCTURE OVERVIEW
1️⃣ Header: Core Invoice Summary (Always Visible)

Layout: Fixed bar at top (or sticky card on scroll)

Element	Example
Customer Name	Acme Design Ltd
Invoice No.	INV-002134
Amount	£12,400.00
Status	🔴 Overdue 14 days
Due Date	20 Sept 2025
Issued Date	20 Aug 2025
Days Overdue	14 days

UX Notes:

Status pill (🟢 Paid / 🟠 Pending / 🔴 Overdue)

Days overdue shown in red badge if >0

Quick “Mark Paid” or “View in Xero” icon buttons

2️⃣ Contact & Client Info Card

Layout: Collapsible section (tap to expand on mobile)

Content:

Contact Name: Sarah Jones

Email: sarah@acmedesign.com
 📧 (tap to email)

Phone: +44 7712 345678 📞 (tap to call)

Address: Pulled from Xero Contact

Credit Limit: £20,000 | Risk Rating: Medium Risk

UX Notes:

Small credit-risk badge (Low / Medium / High)

Tooltip icon with “Last credit check: 14 days ago”

3️⃣ Automation & Schedule Status

Title: “Current Schedule: Standard Reminder Flow”

Content:

Stage: Voice Reminder Scheduled (Day 21)

Next Action: AI Voice Call on Oct 6 @ 10:00 AM

Progress Bar: (Email → SMS → Call → Escalation)

Buttons:

▶ Trigger SMS Now

🎙 Start AI Voice Call

UX Notes:

Each step shows checkmarks or progress indicators

“Pause Schedule” toggle for manual control

📤 Send to Collections

🔗 View in Xero

Mobile: Floating action button (FAB) for “Communicate” opens mini-menu (SMS, Call, Voice).
Desktop: Aligned right, fixed to bottom of the viewport.

🎨 VISUAL DESIGN NOTES

Primary Colour: Qashivo Blue (trust, clarity)

Accent: Gradient green for insurance / finance cards

Typography: Inter or SF Pro — crisp and modern

Icons: Lucide or Feather icons — minimal, consistent

Tone: Confident, financial clarity + tech intelligence
