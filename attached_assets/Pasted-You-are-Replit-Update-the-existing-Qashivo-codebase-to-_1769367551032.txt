DEFINITIONS

A) Invoice lifecycle status (stored, minimal, stable)
invoice_status enum:
- OPEN
- PAID
- VOID
- WRITTEN_OFF

Rules:
- invoice_status is the truth. It does NOT change just because time passes.
- An invoice is “unpaid/open” if invoice_status == OPEN AND balance_due > 0.

B) Collections condition (derived; optionally snapshotted)
collections_condition enum:
- DUE                (more than 7 days before due_date)
- PENDING            (0–7 days before due_date)
- OVERDUE            (0–30 days past due_date)
- CRITICAL           (31–60 days past due_date)
- RECOVERY           (61–90 days past due_date)
- LEGAL              (90+ days past due_date)
- DISPUTED           (override)
- PROMISED           (override; promise_to_pay_date in future)
- PLAN_REQUESTED     (override; request more time/payment plan)

Outcome overlay rules (override precedence, top to bottom):
1) If latest_outcome_type == DISPUTE → collections_condition = DISPUTED
2) Else if latest_outcome_type == PROMISE_TO_PAY AND promise_to_pay_date >= today → PROMISED
3) Else if latest_outcome_type IN (REQUEST_MORE_TIME, PAYMENT_PLAN) → PLAN_REQUESTED
4) Else fall back to age-based ladder derived from due_date.

C) Outcome types (stored as events)
outcome_type enum:
- PROMISE_TO_PAY
- REQUEST_MORE_TIME
- PAYMENT_PLAN
- DISPUTE
- NO_RESPONSE
- OTHER
